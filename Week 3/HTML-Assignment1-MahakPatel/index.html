My Journey as a Corporate Athlete: Balancing Badminton and Career

Published on February 10, 2025 by Mahak Patel

Badminton has always been more than just a sport for me – it's a part of who I am. From a young age, I dreamt of becoming a professional badminton player, and that dream drove me through years of training, competitions, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. But life has a way of taking unexpected turns, and as I entered the corporate world, I found a new challenge: how to balance my career ambitions with my love for badminton.

From College Courts to Corporate Tournaments

My journey as a badminton player began at the inter-college level, where I participated in several tournaments and achieved recognition. I remember the excitement of stepping onto the court, not just for the competition but for the sheer love of the game. Winning those college tournaments was a huge milestone for me, but little did I know, it was only the beginning.

When I started my first job at Accenture, I was determined to keep my passion for badminton alive, despite the demanding corporate environment. Accenture offered me a unique opportunity to participate in corporate tournaments, and I couldn't pass it up. To be honest, juggling work and sport wasn't easy. There were days when the stress of work seemed overwhelming, but the thrill of competing on the badminton court helped me recharge. It gave me a sense of balance – a place where I could push my limits both physically and mentally.

About Mahak Patel

I'm a passionate badminton player and a dedicated professional. I believe that balance, discipline, and determination are the keys to success in both sports and corporate life. When I'm not working, I'm on the court, training for my next tournament.
